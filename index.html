New Technology Challenges:



Games

Games have pretty much dominated a large amount of modern society's entertainment. However, games can also be used to our benefit. By carefully adding reward systems and "gamifying" spaces, games can be used as a whole new form of social connection and motivation.

Watch a Video

Deepfakes

Deepfakes are the ability to realistically render the faces of one person onto another person, using advanced AI face recognization software. This technology will make knowing what is real and what is fake pretty hard, which is pretty scary. I think this technology is something to look out for in the future, especially its use in media.

Watch a Video

AR/VR

Virtual Reality, and its sister Augmented Reality, are two ways to virtually portray software environments. Whereas virtual reality tries to act as a replacement for your senses to fully immerse you into a virtual world, augmented reality simply adds virtual elements overlayed onto your real life experience. I think both of these technologies will be very important in the future, with Augmented Reality probably being the more useful of the two, as it can be integrated into almost any environment.
